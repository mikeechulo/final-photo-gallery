Filmmaking Crew

Filmmaking is a complex and task heavy process so it takes a whole team of talented individuals to be able to create what we know as films or motion pictures. It takes more than technical expertise to produce a film, but brilliant ideas, committed actors, and so on to add passion to the product in order for people to enjoy.

Filmmaking has multiple stages and everyone has a role to fill. It starts with pre-production which includes a writing team and storyboarding to conceptualize the movie before making it. Then the producers will hire the cast and crew since the physical are elements essential. The onset production is where most of the work is done and is arguably the most important stage. And finally, there is post production, such as editing, VFX, sounds, etc., where they refine and complete the final product.
